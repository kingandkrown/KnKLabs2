ECHO GLOVE

The Echo Glove is designed to mirror movement and translate human motion into digital interaction. Built for precision, responsiveness, and intuitive control.

DESIGNED FOR INTUITION

Traditional interfaces create distance between intention and execution. Echo Glove removes that barrier by turning your hand into the interface. Every movement becomes a command. Every gesture becomes communication.

KEY FEATURES

Motion Precision

Advanced sensors capture subtle finger and hand movement for highly accurate interaction.

Real-Time Response

Low latency processing allows seamless translation of motion into digital commands.

Adaptive Design

Built to integrate with creative tools, immersive environments, and future interfaces.

APPLICATIONS

Echo Glove is built for a wide range of environments including creative production, virtual interaction, robotics control, immersive systems, and advanced digital interfaces.
